From https://spicysouthernkitchen.com/overnight-sour-cream-rolls/


Ingredients
===========

    - 115 g salted butter
    - 100 g sugar
    - 225 g sour cream
    - 40 g fresh yeast
    - 140 g water
    - 500-575 g flour
    - 1 teaspoon salt
    - 2 eggs, lightly beaten
    - 1 egg white, whisked until foamy

Instructions 
============

    - Melt butter over low heat. Once melted, stir in sugar, sour cream and salt. Remove from heat and let cool some while you proceed with next step.
    
    - Dissolve yeast in water.
    - Add 500 g flour to a mixing bowl. Add yeast mixture and eggs.
    - Add sour cream mixture and turn mixture on LOW. Once mixed fairly well, turn mixer to MEDIUM and mix for 1 minute.
    - Dough should be soft, not a tight ball, but add a little extra flour (up to 75 g) if too wet
    - Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.
    - Shape into balls about the size of a golfball. You should get 16-20.
    - Place on a baking sheet.
    - Cover and let rise until doubled, 60-90 minutes.
    - Preheat oven to 190 degrees.
    - Brush rolls with beaten egg white.
    - Bake for 14 to 16 minutes or until golden brown.
