Ingredients
===========

- 60 g wheat bran
- 120 g whole-wheat flour
- 100 g granulated sugar
- 1 tsp. baking powder
- 1/2 tsp. baking soda
- 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
- 1 pinch salt
- 250 g unsweetened applesauce
- 2.5 dl buttermilk
- 0.5 dl olive oil
- 1 large egg
- 100 g dried cranberries
- 60 g carrot, grated
- 110 g parsnip, grated
- 2 tsp. finely grated fresh ginger
- 125 g pecans

Preparation
===========

In a medium bowl, combine the wheat bran, flour, sugar, baking powder, soda, cinnamon, and salt.

In a large bowl, combine the applesauce, buttermilk, oil, and egg. Add the flour mixture to the bowl and mix to combine. Fold in the cranberries, carrot, parsnip, and ginger.

Divide the batter among the lined muffin cups, sprinkle with the pecans and bake at 175Â°C until a wooden pick inserted in the middle comes out clean, 25 to 27 minutes. Let the muffins cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
