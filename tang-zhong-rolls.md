Ingredients
===========

**Tangzhong**

- 40 g flour
- 80 g milk
- 80 g water

**Dough**

- 375 g flour
- 25 g yeast
- 8 g salt
- 20 g sugar
- 1 tsp skimmed milk powder
- 1 egg
- 60 g milk
- 20 g water
- 40 g butter (softened)

**Egg wash**

- 1 egg
- 1 tsp cold water
- A pinch of table salt

**To finish**

- 20 g butter (melted)
- Sea salt

Instructions
============

- Prepare tang zhong in microwave. Let cool.
- Dissolve yeast in lukewarm milk and water.
- Add sugar, salt, milk powder and tang zhong.
- Add in flour. Mix for 5 minutes.
- Add softened butter, mix for 5-10 minutes.
- Let dough rise to double size (45-90 minutes).
- Divide dough into 12 pieces and place in a baking tin.
- Let rolls prove form 45-60 minutes until doubled in size.
- Brush with egg mix.
- Bake at 160 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
- Brush with melted butter.
